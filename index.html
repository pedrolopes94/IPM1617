This is a project for a Human–Computer Interaction course in FCT NOVA.

Our goal is to develop a camping trip planner application which replaces the spreadsheet when it comes to dealing with camping planning.

Although there are many apps for planning trips, we could only find one specific app for planning camping activities and in turn, it hasn't the best visual presentation.
