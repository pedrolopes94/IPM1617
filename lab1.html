Monefy

The objective of this interface is to track the money of a user, more specifically his expenses.
Basically, the user adds an expense associated with a category, and then the user can view his expenses in different formats.


The good aspects of this app are its intuition and a has a very simple, yet elegant design.
It can also be easily custmoized and if any data is incorrectely inputed it can easily be updated.


Monefy




The objective of this interface is to identify your personality type.
Basically, the user answers to a certain number of questions and in the end he/she gets a result that corresponds better to his/her type of personality, between 16 possible types of personality.


The good aspects of this app is the good looking and elegant design, resorting to images to represent the several types of personalities. The good using of colors and sizes of the answers is also a good point for the design.

Trello

The objective of this interface is to manage a small team or project.
The user can add projects, add people to projects, add tasks and view its evolution, and a lot more.


Good aspects of this app are its customizability, how it shows the progress of a project, and its global interactivity between project participants and the community outside the project.

HumanMetrics

The objective of this interface is the same as 16Personalities application, to identify the personality type that corresponds better to the user.


The bad aspects of this app are its presentation.
The way the questions are shown to the user, as well as the final result, it is not very pleasant to read. Also, the 16 types of personality are more difficult to read and understand their meanings (they are represented as acronyms), unlike the 16Personalities app which uses images to explain the types of personality.


HumanMetrics




Right-handed desks

The objective of this interface is for students to sit and take classes or exams.


The bad aspects of this user interface are the comfortability and usability.
It is very hard for left handed people to use this kind of chairs, and it is very hard to have multiple sheets parallel to each other in a same stand.
